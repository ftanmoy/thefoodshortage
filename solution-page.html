How to Help

World Central Kitchen is a non-profit organization whose goal is to provide meals to those who are struggling during natural disasters. In this current crisis of COVID-19, they have already

  • Provided over 35 million meals in more than 400 cities
  • Purchased over 12 million meals from local restaurants totaling more than $135 million

Not only are they distributing food to those who are in need, they are lifting the economy by keeping restaurants in service.
